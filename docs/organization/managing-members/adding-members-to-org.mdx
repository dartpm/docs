As an organisation owner, you can add other dartpm users to your organisation to give them read or read and write access to publi and private packages within your organisation's scope, as well as public unscoped packages governed by your organisation.

When you add a member to your organisation, they can see the invite in organisation's list.

Once the new member accepts the invitation, they are:
- assigned the role of "viewer"
- added to the "developers" team

If you have a paid organisation, as part of the dartpm Organisation plan, you will be billed $12 per month for each nnew member. 

## Inviting members to yoir organisation
1. On the dartpm ["Sign In"](https://dartpm.com/login) page, enter your account details and click Sign In.

<img src={loginSignupImg} width="500"/>

2. In the upper right corner of the page, click your profile picture, then click Account.

3. In the left sidebar, click the name of your organisation.

4. On the organisation settings page, click Members.

5. Click the Invite Members button.

6. In the "Username or email" field, type the username or email address of the person you wiish to invite.

7. Click Invite

## Revoking an organisation invitation

As an organisation owner, if you've made a mistake in inviting someone to your organisation, you can revoke the organisation invitiation. 

1. On the dartpm ["Sign In"](https://dartpm.com/login) page, enter your account details and click Sign In.

<img src={loginSignupImg} width="500"/>

2. In the upper right corner of the page, click your profile picture, then click Account.

3. In the left sidebar, click the name of your organisation.

4. On the organisation settings page, click Members.

5. Under the "Invitations" field, click the X next to the name of the user invitation you would like to revoke.
